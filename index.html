About

Welcome to lifehappens, founded by a resilient 25-year-old woman on a mission to raise awareness about mental health and chronic medical conditions through the power of stickers. As someone who has personally faced the challenges of living with various mental and physical conditions, she understands the importance of destigmatizing these topics and fostering understanding and empathy. We believe in the transformative power of stickers as vehicles for education, expression, and advocacy.

"Life may throw obstacles in your path and unexpected challenges may arise, but it is through these moments that your strength is truly forged. Embrace the hurdles, for they are the catalysts of growth and resilience. Remember, when life happens, it's not about how it knocks you down, but how it empowers you to rise stronger than ever before."

Our Mission

At lifehappens, we believe in the power of stickers to uplift, educate, and promote well-being. Our collection includes a dedicated range of stickers that shine a light on mental health awareness, self-care, and medical conditions. From inspiring quotes and affirmations to whimsical graphics, our stickers are designed to spark conversations, spread positivity, and provide a sense of support.

We understand the importance of representing and addressing mental and physical health topics with sensitivity and accuracy. That's why our stickers are thoughtfully crafted. We strive to create a safe and inclusive space where individuals can find solace, share their stories, and embrace their journeys.

In addition to our mental and physical health condition stickers, we offer a diverse range of designs to cater to every taste and interest. From pop culture references and trendy aesthetics to nature-inspired motifs and more, you're sure to find stickers that speak to your unique personality and style.

Our commitment to quality means that each sticker is made using premium materials, ensuring durability and vibrant colors that withstand the test of time. Whether you choose to adorn your laptop, water bottle, journal, or any other surface, our stickers are crafted to make a lasting impression.

Join us in our mission to raise awareness and promote understanding. Together, let's create a world where mental health and chronic medical conditions are met with compassion and acceptance. Browse our collection, find stickers that resonate with you, and become a part of the movement for change.

We're not just passionate about stickers; we're also dedicated to making a positive impact on the world. That's why we have incorporated a charitable giving program into our business model. We're proud to share that a percentage of the profits from our Awareness Sticker line will go to various charities.

Remember, every sticker you buy is a small step towards making a big impact. Happy sticker shopping!
